About the course

Generative AI for Work & Research

This course explores practical ways to use generative AI and no-code AI tools to support professional tasks, research workflows, writing, productivity, communication, and evidence-informed work.

✦

Research productivity

Using AI to structure literature notes, summarize evidence, organize ideas, and support clearer research communication.

⌁

Work automation

Exploring no-code tools that can reduce repetitive tasks, improve planning, and support efficient professional workflows.

◈

Responsible practice

Building careful habits around accuracy, privacy, transparency, human judgement, and ethical use of AI-generated outputs.
